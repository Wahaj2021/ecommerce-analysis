# E-Commerce Sales Analysis (Python & Power BI)

##  Business Problem

E-commerce businesses rely heavily on data to optimise revenue, customer retention, and product performance. 

This project analyses transactional data to identify sales trends, top-performing products, customer behaviour patterns, and revenue drivers.

---

##  Objectives

- Analyse overall revenue performance
- Identify top-performing products and categories
- Evaluate seasonal sales trends
- Understand customer purchasing behaviour
- Build a KPI dashboard for business monitoring

---

## Tools Used

- **Python:** Pandas, NumPy
- **Visualisation:** Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **Power BI:** Interactive dashboard, DAX measures

##  Key Insights

- Revenue shows seasonal peaks during specific months.
- A small percentage of products generate the majority of revenue (Pareto effect).
- Repeat customers contribute significantly to total sales.
- Certain product categories consistently outperform others.
